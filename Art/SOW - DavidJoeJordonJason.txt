Statement of Work (S.O.W.)
What I want as the bare minimum for the game

-3 Different COM cars
-1 or 2 tracks
-AI for the COM players
-4 power ups (health up, rocket, oil slick, ice)
-Stats upgradable and mod shop (customizable cars)
-A garage, allowing the player to have up to 4 cars
-Menus for Main, Upgrades, Car select, Track select
-HUD for racing
-XBOX Controller Compatable

-4 tracks (enough for a GP)
-As many powerups as we can think of (there should probably be a limit of ~10)
-A larger garage
-A super car that is purchasable (it has more max points)
-Kickass music
